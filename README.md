# 🚀NPM Youtube Automation 

Welcome to the future of content creation! This tool combines the power of **AI-generated metadata** with **automatic YouTube uploading**. Non-technical creators can now upload videos with smart titles, descriptions, and hashtags — all in one click.

## To understand repo project with AI in detail with full documentation visit here:-
[![Ask DeepWiki](https://deepwiki.com/badge.svg)](https://deepwiki.com/sonuramashishnpm/NPM-Youtube-Automation)

## ✨ Features

- **AI-Generated Titles**: Catchy, SEO-friendly titles generated automatically.
- **Smart Video Descriptions**: Engaging descriptions that summarize content and boost discoverability.
- **Viral-Ready Hashtags**: Trending hashtags automatically suggested for better reach.
- **Automatic YouTube Upload**: Upload videos directly to your channel with AI metadata applied.

## ⚡ How It Works

1. Provide your video file (MP4 or similar).  
2. AI generates **title, description, and hashtags** automatically.  
3. Authenticates securely with your YouTube account.  
4. Uploads video automatically, showing progress and final video ID.

## 🛠 No Technical Knowledge Needed

- No command-line expertise required.  
- No API key management for end users.  
- Fully automated workflow: just select a video and press upload.

> **Pro Tip:** Future versions will integrate cloud uploads — record on your phone and upload instantly via AI!
